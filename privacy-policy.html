OCHE — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 June 2026

This page is the public-facing privacy policy for the OCHE — Dart Scorer mobile app (iOS, Android, web). It mirrors the in-app policy accessible from Settings → Legal → Privacy Policy.

Contact: maieul.lp@gmail.com

Data we collect

OCHE is an offline-first application. All gameplay data — scores, player profiles, settings, achievements, game history, replays — is stored locally on your device (IndexedDB on web, the OS keychain/preferences on native). None of it is sent to any server. No account is required to use the app.

In-app purchases

If you purchase the Premium upgrade, the transaction is processed by Apple App Store (on iOS) or Google Play (on Android). We use RevenueCat to verify your Premium entitlement across platforms.

RevenueCat receives an anonymous purchase identifier — not your name, email, or any contact information.

Advertising

Free users may see rewarded video and interstitial ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect a device advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android) and information about your device to personalise the ads shown.

You can opt out of personalised ads at any time:

  • iOS — Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → toggle off OCHE
  • Android — Settings → Privacy → Ads → opt out of ad personalisation

Premium users see zero ads. AdMob is not initialised at all on Premium accounts.

Children's privacy

OCHE is rated 4+ / Everyone and does not knowingly collect personal data from any user, including children. The offline-first design means no data leaves the device.

Your rights

You can delete all your OCHE data at any time by uninstalling the app. No copies are kept on our servers — because there are no servers. There is nothing for us to delete on our side.

To object to AdMob personalisation, use the OS-level controls listed in the Advertising section above.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, the in-app version's "Last updated" footer will reflect the new date. The latest version is always available at this URL.

Frequently asked questions

I bought Premium but it's not active on my other device

Open OCHE → Settings → tap "Restore Purchases". Apple / Google will re-validate your Premium entitlement. The same purchase covers up to five family members via Family Sharing on iOS.

I want a refund

Refunds are handled by the platform you bought from, not by us. On iOS: reportaproblem.apple.com. On Android: Play Store → Order history → Request refund. We can answer questions but cannot refund directly.

Ads are not playing on my device

Make sure you have an internet connection. Ad-blockers or restricted-tracking settings can prevent ad delivery. If you saw no ads at all on your first launch, please contact us — your device may have hit Google's anti-fraud heuristic. Premium removes all ads regardless.

My stats / profiles disappeared after reinstalling

OCHE stores all data locally on your device (no cloud account). Uninstalling clears game history, profiles and achievements. On Android the system backup may restore them; on iOS, iCloud backup restores them only if the device was restored from backup. Premium status itself is always recoverable via "Restore Purchases".

Which modes are free vs Premium ?

The X01 family (301, 501, 701, 901, 301 Double) and Cricket (Classic + Cut-Throat) and Training are free forever, unlimited. The "fun modes" (Killer, Hunter, Shanghai, Gotcha, Around the Clock, Halve It, and 11 others) give you 3 free games before you need Premium or watch an ad.

Bug report / feature request

Email ocheapp.contact@gmail.com with what happened, the mode you were playing, and a screenshot if possible. We read everything.
